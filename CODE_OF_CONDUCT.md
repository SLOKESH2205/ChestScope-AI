# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the community.
- Show empathy towards other community members.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project manager.
